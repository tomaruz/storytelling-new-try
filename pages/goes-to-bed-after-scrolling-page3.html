After a while of scrolling online, Noah finally decides to go to sleep.
He puts his phone down and closes his eyes.
However, he finds himself struggling to fall asleep.
DID YOU KNOW THAT...

6 in 10 Britons usually look at their mobile devices
shortly before bed.

Looking at your phone before bed lowers the chances of getting a good night's sleep as the blue light emitted by your cell phone screen restrains the production of melatonin, the hormone that controls your sleep-wake cycle. This makes it difficult to fall asleep and wake up the next day.

Eventually, after a long struggle, Noah finally falls asleep.
But soon his alarm rings.
Noah wakes up feeling tired.
DID YOU KNOW THAT...

If Noah doesn't change his bad sleeping habits and continues getting less than the recommended 7-9 hours of sleep, he will begin experiencing symptoms such as:

Slowed thinking
Reduced attention span
Worsened memory
Poor or risky decision-making
Lack of energy
Mood changes, including feelings of stress, anxiety, or irritability.
